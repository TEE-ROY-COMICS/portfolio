About me

Hi, my name is Tanmoy Roy.

I'm a current student studying communication design at Hochschule Mannheim in Germany. I’m specializing in UX design and want to work in that field after my bachelor/master. Furthermore, I also draw, write, sell comics and make commissions on the side. Here’s my other website if you want to keep track with my other works.

If I had to describe myself in 3 words regarding work, I would answer: creative, organized and productive.

What do I love the most about UX? The methodology behind it. To be creative in my future carrier was a requirement that was really important to me. But just creativeness isn't enough if you can't justify it. That's where the all the guidelines and psychology behind UX comes in handy. They will give you actual arguments to back up your designs, ensuring not only good looking but also objectively liked works. Design with a purpose is good design!
