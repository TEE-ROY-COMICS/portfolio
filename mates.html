In a nutshell

mates is a mockup of a communication app that groups people together according to their interests in hobbies with the catch that their interactions are anonymous and with locals. This project was developed for a university project by a team comprised of five communication design students from Hochschule Mannheim.

Retrospective

This was the first time I’ve worked on an UX project and also the first one in a team, so naturally I’ve learned a lot like distributing tasks and the know-how of all the new programs we used.
